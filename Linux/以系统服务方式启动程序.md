### systemd 和 init.d 之间的区别

```
Sysvinit

-is more closely related to Unix philosophy and design

-Starts processes sequentially(ish)

-Older than Systemd

Systemd

-not POSIX spirited

-Starts processes in parallel

-Adopted by most major Linux based distributions
```
